Secreted by [[Foveolar Cell]] and [[Mucous Neck Cell]]

# Types
There are 2 types of gastric mucous

## Insoluble Mucus
**Basic** and **thick** -> **protection** against [[Gastric Secretion]]

## Soluble Mucus
Lubricate [[Chyme]]

# Control
## Stimulate
- **[[CN X]]**
- **[[Secretin]]**, **[[Prostaglandins]]**, etc
- Irritation 

## Inhibit
Mainly [[NSAIDs]]
