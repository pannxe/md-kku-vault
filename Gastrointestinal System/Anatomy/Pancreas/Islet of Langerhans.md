# Cells
## Principle
- **[[α Cell]]** → **[[Glucagon]]** -- 30-50%; at periphery
- **[[β Cell]]** → **[[Insulin]]** and [[Amylin]] -- **50-60%**; in the middle
- **[[δ Cell]]** → **[[Somatostatin]]** -- ~5%

## Minor
Less than 1% of all cell
- [[γ Cell]] → [[Pancreatic Polypeptide]]
- [[ε Cell]] → [[Ghrelin]]
- [[D1 Cell]] → [[VIP]]
- [[EC Cell]] → [[Secretin]], [[Motilin]], and [[Substance P]]
