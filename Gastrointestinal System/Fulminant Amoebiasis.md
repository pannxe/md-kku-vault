A complication of [[Amoebic Dysentery]] (very rare, < 0.5%). **Mortality rate < 40%**

# Risk Factors
- [[Malnutrition]] 
- [[Pregnancy]]
- [[Corticosteroid]] use
- Young age

# Manifestation
Rapid onset of
- Severe [[Bloody Diarrhoea]]
- Severe [[Abdominal Pain]]
- [[Peritonitis]]
- [[Fever]]
- [[Intestinal Perforation]]
- [[Toxic Megacolon]] <- [[Corticosteroid]] use
