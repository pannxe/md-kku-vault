# Symptom
- [[Nausea and Vomitting]]
- Rt-upper quadrant [[Abdominal Pain]]
- [[Jaundice]]
May include :
- [[Weight Loss]]
- [[Fatigue]]
- [[Weakness]]

- ***[[Opisthorchis viverrini]]***

## Large Liver Fluke
- ***[[Fasciola gigantic]]***
