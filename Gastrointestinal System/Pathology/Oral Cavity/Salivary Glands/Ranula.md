# Pathogenesis
Similar to **[[Mucocoele]]** but usually at the [[Salivary Ducts]] of **[[Submandibular Gland]]** or **[[Sublingual Gland]]**

# Forms

- **Cervical** (**Plunging**) **ranula** -- between [[Mylohyoideus]] and below space -> **swelling of [[Neck]]**
