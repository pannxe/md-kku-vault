Inflammation of [[Gingiva]]

# Pathogenesis
Poor oral hygiene -> [[Dental Plaque]] and [[Calculus]] -> **plaque build-up under [[Gingiva]]** -> inflammation

# Clinical Manifestation
- **[[Oedema]]**
- **[[Erythema]] -> [[Gingival Bleeding]]**
- Change in colours

# Treatment 
- Oral hygiene
- [[Dental Scaling]]
