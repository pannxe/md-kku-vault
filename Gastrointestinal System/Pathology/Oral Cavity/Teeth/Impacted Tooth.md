Nearby structures -> **disturbance of teeth eruption** esp. at [[3rd Molar]]

# Complication
- **[[Pericornitis]]**
- Accumulation of food -> **[[Dental Caries]]** -> [[Periodontal Diseases]]
- Pressure from [[3rd Molar]] -> 
	- **Damage to root of nearby teeth**
	- **Crowding teeth** 
- **[[Dentigerous Cyst]]**

# Management
[[Tooth Extraction]] or surgery
