# Life Cycle
Normally infects [[Rodents]].
IH -- **[[Beetle]]** (including [[Cockchafer]]), **[[Fleas]]**, and **[[Cockroach]]**

# Clinical Manifestation
**Asymptomatic**

# Diagnosis
**Egg in stool**, **NO polar filament**
> See [[Hymenolepis nana]] for diffences

# Treatement

- [[Niclosamide]]
