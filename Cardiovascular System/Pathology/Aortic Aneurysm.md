# Abdominal

![[Pasted image 20220627201156.png]]

Usually occur btwn [[Renal Artery]] and [[Aorta#Bifurcation]]

## Risk Factor
Men and **[[Tabacco]]**

## Pathogenesis
Complication from **[[Atherosclerosis]]** → inflammation → **落 [[Macrophage]]** →  ↑↑ **[[Metalloprotease]]**

## Consequences
- **Compression** on nearby structure (e.g., [[Ureter]]) →
	- **Obstruction** of [[Renal Artery]], [[Common Iliac Artery]], [[Inferior Mesenteric Artery]], etc.
	- Erosion of [[Vertebrae]], etc.
- **[[Embolism]]** -- atheromatous material
- **Rupture** -- massive haemorrahge → **fatal**

> Visible pulsating abdominal mass

## Risks
Risk of rupture **d size** 
- < 4 cm. -- rarely rupture
- > 6 cm. -- 25% per year 

Mortality :
- **Elective Sx** -- **> 5%**
- **Emergency Sx** -- **50%**

# Thoracic
assoc. **[[Hypertension]]**, **[[Marfan Syndrome]]**, and [[Bicuspid Aortic Valves]]

## Sign and Symptoms
- **[[Dyspnoea]]** and **[[Dysphagia]]** -- compression → obstruction
- **Persistent [[Cough]]** -- irritation to [[Recurrent Laryngeal Nerve]]
- Pain -- erosion of bones
- [[Valvular Heart Disease]] -- [[Aortic Valve]] insufficiency

→ **[[Aortic Dissection]]** -- complication
