a.k.a. **[[Angiotensin II Receptor]]** [[Neprilysin]] Inhinitor, LCZ696, Entresto

i.e., [[Valsartan]] + [[Sacubitril]]

# Mechanism of Action
** breakdown of [[Neprilysin]]** → 
- **↑ [[Natriuretic Peptides]]** (ANP, BNP)
- ↑ [[Bradykinin]]
- ↑ [[Angiotensin II]] ⊢ [[Valsartan]]

→ **[[Diuresis]]** and **[[Vasodilation]]**

# Indication
**[[Heart Failure]]**

# Side Effects
- [[Hypotension]]
- [[Hyperkalaemia]]
- [[Cough]] and [[Dizziness]]
- [[Renal Failure]]
