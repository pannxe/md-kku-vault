# Pathogenesis
**[[Diabete Mellitus]]** → ↓ Glu utilization in [[Brain]] → **↑ [[Ketogenesis]]** at [[Liver]] (2nd source for brain) → ↓ blood pH (← acidic by-product) → **[[Acidosis]]**

- **[[Abdominal Pain]]**
