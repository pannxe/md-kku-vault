**Nitrix Oxide**
- Synthesized from [[Guanidium Nitrogen]] of [[Arginine]] by [[NO Synthase]]
- Diffuse to cytoplasm and act on [[NO Receptor]] -> act on [[Guanylyl Cyclase]] -> **↑ [[cGMP]]** -> **↓ cytosolic Ca2+** -> Vasodilation, etc.
> See [[Signal Transduction]]
