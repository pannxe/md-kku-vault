**Desmopressin Acetate** (dDVP, minirin)

# Mechanism of Action
Modified [[ADH]] analog → **selective [[V2 Receptor]] agonist** → **落 [[Renal Tubular Cells]]** → ↑ [[Na-Water Retention]]
> 4000x more potent q [[ADH]]

# Indications
**[[Diabetes Insipidus]]**

# Side Effects
- **[[Overhydration]]** → **[[Hyponatraemia]]**
- [[Hypertension]]
- [[Convulsion]], etc.
