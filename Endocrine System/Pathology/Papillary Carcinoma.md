- **Most common malignant [[Thyroid Neoplasms]]**
- Female ~40 y/o

# Pathogenesis
- [[RET gene]] or [[NTRK1]] mutation → abnormal **[[Receptor Tyrosine Kinase]]**
- [[BRAF]] mutation
