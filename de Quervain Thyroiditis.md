# Cause

# Clinical Manifestation
- More common in young female
- **[[Sore Thoat]]**, **[[Pain]]**, and **Flu-like symptoms**
- **[[Thyroid Gland]] enlargement**
- **Self-limited** in 6-8 wks.
- **Transient [[Hyperthyroidism]]**
