## Autoregulation
Autoregulation in [[Brain]] is mostly depends on **metabolite**, i.e., **PO2**, **PCO2**, **H+ conc.**, and **metabolite from [[Astrocyte]]** → ↑↑ or ↓↓ in [[Blood Pressure]] → ←→ cerebral blood flow

However, it only **effective around [[MAP]] ~50-150 mmHg**
