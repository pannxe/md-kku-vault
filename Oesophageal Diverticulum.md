Pouch of [[Oesophagus]]; there are 3 common types 

# Zenker Diverticulum
- At **proximal oesophagus**
- **Caused by** : **dysfuction of [[Cricopharyngeus]]** -> dysfunction [[Upper Oesophageal Sphincter]] -> **stress at oesophageal wall during [[Swallowing]]** 
	
# Traction Diverticulum
- At **middle oesophagus**
- Caused by : **[[Fibrosis]] of [[Mediastinum]]**
	
# Epiphrenic Diverticulum
- At **distal oesophagus**
- Caused by : **dyscoordination of [[Peristalsis]]** and [[Lower Oesophageal Sphincter]] relaxation -> **strees at oesophageal wall**

# Clincal Manifestation
- [[Regurgitation]]
- [[Aspiration Pneumonia]]
- [[Dysphagia]], [[Bad Breath]] <- decomposed food
- Large Zenker -> mass at [[Neck]] -> compression

# Diagnosis
[[Oesophagogram]] c [[Barium Contrast]]
